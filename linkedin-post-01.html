Your lab could have 200 pieces of glassware…

But if you're missing even ONE of these 5 types, your results are at risk.

Most labs accumulate glassware over time without auditing whether they actually have the right types for their workflows.

The result? Inaccurate measurements, contamination risks, and wasted resources.

Here are the 5 non-negotiable glassware types every lab must have:

  • Beakers — for mixing, heating, and general-purpose use
  • Volumetric Flasks — for preparing precise standard solutions
  • Erlenmeyer Flasks — for titrations and swirling without spills
  • Graduated Cylinders — for accurate volume measurement
  • Pipettes — for transferring exact small volumes

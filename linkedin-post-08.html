Shipping glass across the world sounds like a nightmare.

Here's how we do it — with a near-zero breakage rate.

International buyers often worry about one thing above all: "Will my glassware arrive intact?"

It's a valid concern. Fragile goods + long-distance shipping = risk. But with the right process, it's a solvable problem.

Our export process — from factory to your lab door:

  • Individual wrapping — each piece is wrapped in foam and bubble cushioning
  • Custom carton sizing — no excess space means no movement during transit
  • Double-walled boxes — heavy-duty corrugated cartons for outer protection
  • Export documentation — MSDS, packing lists, certificates of conformity included
  • Trusted freight partners — we work with vetted shipping lines for reliable delivery
  • Customs support — we assist with import clearance documentation
Medilab Exports has shipped to labs, distributors, and hospitals in over 35 countries across Asia, Africa, Europe, and the Americas — with consistently high delivery satisfaction.

Most buyers never see what happens between "order placed" and "shipment received."

Here's our complete supply chain — with nothing hidden.

In laboratory equipment, supply chain transparency isn't just nice to have — it's a trust factor.

Buyers want to know: who made this, how it was tested, and how it got to my door.

Our end-to-end supply chain — 7 stages from glass to lab:

  • Stage 1: Raw material sourcing — high-grade borosilicate glass from certified suppliers
  • Stage 2: Manufacturing — precision glassblowing and moulding in our facility
  • Stage 3: Annealing — controlled cooling to eliminate internal stress
  • Stage 4: Calibration — individual testing against ISO tolerances
  • Stage 5: Quality inspection — visual, dimensional, and functional checks
  • Stage 6: Packaging — export-grade wrapping and boxing
  • Stage 7: Shipping & documentation — freight booking, customs paperwork, tracking
At Medilab Exports, we control every stage in-house — no outsourced middlemen. This means consistent quality, faster turnaround, and full traceability on every shipment.

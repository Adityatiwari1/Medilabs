If your research lab had to start from scratch…

These are the instruments you'd buy on Day 1.

Setting up a research lab — or restocking one — can be overwhelming. There are hundreds of instruments to choose from.

But experienced lab managers know: a few core instruments handle 80% of daily workflows.

The instruments every research lab relies on daily:

  • Beakers & Flasks — the workhorses of mixing, heating, and reactions
  • Burettes — essential for titration and precise dispensing
  • Pipettes — for exact volume transfers in analytical work
  • Condensers — critical for distillation and reflux setups
  • Graduated Cylinders — reliable volume measurement for everyday tasks
  • Desiccators — for moisture-free storage of hygroscopic samples
  • Petri Dishes — the backbone of microbiology and cell culture work
